on·o·mas·tics
ˌänəˈmastiks,ˌônəˈmastiks/
noun
the study of the history and origin of proper names, especially personal names.


"Lately my preoccupation with names has become an obsession. To make my pedestrian interest sound more scholarly and pedantic I have used the sociological term Onomastics as my topic of research. It means, the science of studying naming patterns.

The problem I encountered with names in old colonial Philippines was a pain in the neck.
