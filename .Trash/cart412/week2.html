23-3d-beach-sand-wallpaper by Julieta Gil - This is a simple physical manipulation of an object, in this case, a bedroom set, where the user is seen both inside and outside. Narrative Clip Wearable Camera - This camera allows the user to simply clip this pin on their shirt and enjoy thier day while the device takes random snapshots.
